Ionic Gestures

  • Support for `tap`, `double-tap`, `hold` 
    <canvas on-tap="onTap($event)"
        on-double-tap="onDoubleTap()
        on-hold="onHold()"></canvas>
  • Support for `drag`, `swipe` 
    <canvas on-drag="onDrag($event)"
        on-swipe="onSwipe()
        on-swipe-left="onSwipeLeft()"></canvas>
